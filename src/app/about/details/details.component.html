about us

NOMAD is the first prototype of its kind in the world to serve as an integrated development with combined centres of excellence for entrepreneurship, media, culinary arts, makerspace, gaming and technology.

Operating as a global membership model, the NOMAD community will have access to production studios, co-working and co-housing spaces, events and accelerator programmes on site along with a network of mentors, investors and industry partners.

Located near Dubai Outlet Mall, the comprehensive development by Al Ahli Holding Group comprises of a 1 million square foot infrastructure.
